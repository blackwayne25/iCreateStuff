iCreateStuff – AI-Powered Autonomous Content Creator

Overview:
iCreateStuff is a revolutionary React Native app that transforms content creation into a fully autonomous, AI-driven experience. Designed for creators, entrepreneurs, and teams, the app combines dynamic AI voices, interactive animations, real-time video collaboration, and automated storytelling pipelines to deliver a complete creative ecosystem — all guided by BigDes, your AI creative overseer.

Key Features:

1. AI Roundtable 2.0

Three AI roles — Creator, Entrepreneur, Executive — collaborate, debate, and vote on content ideas in real-time.

Role-based TTS voices provide unique, immersive commentary for every AI action.

BigDes overlays guide users through every stage, providing insights and creative prompts.


2. Reactive Lottie Animations

AI actions dynamically trigger Lottie animations per role.

Idle, action, and decision animations give the roundtable a live, cinematic feel.

Fully mobile/tablet responsive — animations scale seamlessly to any device.


3. Real-Time Video Collaboration

Agora integration for live video feeds in the Main Room.

Executive Room privacy fully protected — video only activates with proper permissions.

Ideal for co-creation sessions with your team or collaborators.


4. Autonomous Idea Generation

AI roles automatically generate innovative content ideas without manual input.

Ideas feed directly into storyboarding and animation pipelines via placeholder Sora and Meta API hooks.

BigDes narrates each step, ensuring clarity and a creative feedback loop.
